The ICE24 Encoders produce QSIF to full resolution
MPEG2 and MPEG4 compliant files. The ICE24 will produce
MPEG2 files from 500kps to 15Mbps and MPEG4 files from 20kps
to 4Mbps in both constant and variable bit rate encoding with
closed captioning support.
 
The ICE24 also supports serial digital and up to 8 channels of
analog or digital  audio as well as the encoding of embedded
audio in the SDI stream and includes a DVB output
for realtime transmission of MPEG files while encoding.
ICE Broadcast encoders will provide the necessary storage
and power to encode form programming, meeting.
 
Multiple encoders with real-time confidence monitors can be installed
and multi-input encoders are available without the real
time confidence monitor option. Automated encoding is also
available using ICE Broadcasts automation system to
streamline workflow and reduce man-hours.
