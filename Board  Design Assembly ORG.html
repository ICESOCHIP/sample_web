ICE Team expertise in working with engineering groups implementing the design, building prototypes, and providing  documentation is valuable decreasing your design cycle and getting your design  to market sooner with a reliable product.
We work with startups, running at full speed is normal when the design is still in the final Stage. And also, working with very large companies, interfacing with multiple groups within the customer organization, keeping the project on track, ICE bridges the gap between engineering and operations. With the right team, tools, equipment, and experience to get the design delivered in time-to-market .

Most people have the manufacturing equipment, but very few actually have a good materials team that performs well on quick-turns. Our materials team closely interfaces with our in-house design team during the design phase to identify lead-time parts, recommend packages readily available,and raise the flag on EOL parts. Our process is setup to track the daily changes made by engineering during the design phase, and transmit the BOM changes to the materials team. This close coordination is key to successful delivery of quick-turn prototypes and a smooth transition into production.

We work with premier PCB houses locally to build your printed circuit boards. With a well-defined model in place to quickly identify the right fit based on your technology, turnaround, and quantity,  For more simple "vanilla" boards, we marry them up with a more cost effective Tier 2 or 3 "proven" source while high-end boards will go to our high-end Tier 1 suppliers. We have built PCB's with extreme complex parameters and have a very close working relationship with  suppliers.
